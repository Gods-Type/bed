### Right Click - Not In Tab
Right Clicking on the Tab in the Tab Panel or in Open Editor at the top the editor will open up a context menu with the following options:
- Common
  - Reload Tab
  - Copy Tab Link
- Rename
  - Rename Grp (which will rename the Grp)
  - Rename Space (which will rename the Space)
- Close
  - Close Tab (which will close the Tab)
  - Close Grp (which will close the Grp and the Tabs inside the Grp)
  - Close Space (which will close the Grps and the Tabs inside the Grps)
  - Close Window (which will close the Spaces and the Grps and the Tabs inside the Grps)
- Duplicate
  - Duplicate Tab (which will duplicate the Tab)
  - Duplicate Grp (which will duplicate the Grp and the Tabs inside the Grp)
  - Duplicate Space (which will duplicate the Grps and the Tabs inside the Grps)
  - Duplicate Window (which will duplicate the Spaces and the Grps and the Tabs inside the Grps)
- Archive
  - Archive Tab (which will archive the Tab)
  - Archive Grp (which will archive the Grp and the Tabs inside the Grp)
  - Archive Space (which will archive the Grps and the Tabs inside the Grps)
  - Archive Window (which will archive the Spaces and the Grps and the Tabs inside the Grps)
- Restore
  - Restore Tab (which will restore the Tab)
  - Restore Grp (which will restore the Grp and the Tabs inside the Grp)
  - Restore Space (which will restore the Grps and the Tabs inside the Grps)
  - Restore Window (which will restore the Spaces and the Grps and the Tabs inside the Grps)
- Open
  - Open New Tab 
  - Open Split View With New Tab 
  - Open Split View With Current Tab 
- Add
  - Add Tab To Grp (which will add to existing Grp)
  - Add New Tab To Grp
  - Add As New Grp (which will create a Grp)
  - Add New Space (which will create a Space)
- Move To
  - Move Tab to New Grp
  - Move Tab To New Space
  - Move Tab To New Window
  - Move Tab To Existing Grp
  - Move Tab To Existing Space
  - Move Tab To Existing Window
  - Move Grp To New Space
  - Move Grp To New Window
  - Move Grp To Existing Grp
  - Move Grp To Existing Space
  - Move Grp To Existing Window
  - Move Space To New Window
  - Move Space To Existing Space
  - Move Space To Existing Window
- Sync
  - Sync Tab (which will sync the Tab)
  - Sync Grp (which will sync the Grp and the Tabs inside the Grp)
  - Sync Space (which will sync the Space and the Grps inside the Space and the Tabs inside the Grps)
  - Sync Window (which will sync the Spaces and the Grps inside the Spaces and the Tabs inside the Grps)
- Pin
  - Pin Tab (which will pin the Tab)
  - Pin Grp (which will pin the Grp and the Tabs inside the Grp)
  - Pin Space (which will pin the Space and the Grps and the Tabs inside the Grp)
  - Pin Window (which will pin the Spaces the Grps inside the Spaces and the Tabs inside the Grps)
- Mute
  - Mute Tab (which will mute the Tab)
  - Mute Grp (which will mute the Grp and the Tabs inside the Grp)
  - Mute Space (which will mute the Space and the Grps inside the Space and the Tabs inside the Grps)
  - Mute Window (which will mute the Spaces and the Grps inside the Spaces and the Tabs inside the Grps)
- Collapse all
